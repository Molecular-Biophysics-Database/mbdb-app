The Molecular Biophysics Database is a comprehensive resource for investigating biomolecular structure and dynamics. It provides researchers with access to experimental and computational data on molecular interactions, conformational changes, energetics, and functional properties. This repository encompasses a wide range of biomolecules, enabling in-depth analyses of molecular mechanisms and their implications in biological processes. The user-friendly interface and comprehensive data annotations make it a valuable tool for drug discovery, protein engineering, and understanding cellular processes at the atomic level.
